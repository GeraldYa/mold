# User Information — Developer Portfolio

Please answer these questions. Short answers are fine.

1. What is your full name?
2. What's your one-line title? (e.g. "Full-Stack Developer", "AI Engineer", "Designer & Builder")
3. Email address?
4. GitHub URL? (optional)
5. LinkedIn URL? (optional)
6. Other links? (portfolio, blog, Twitter, etc.)
7. Do you have a resume PDF to link? (yes/no, filename if yes)

8. List 2-4 key numbers about yourself:
   (e.g. "5 years experience", "10 projects shipped", "3 languages spoken")

9. Tell your career story in 3-5 bullet points, one per stage:
   (e.g. "2018 - Started as intern at Google", "2020 - Led a team of 5", "Now - Building my own startup")

10. List your projects (at least 3, up to 6):
    For each: name, tech stack, one-sentence description, link (GitHub/demo/etc.)

11. List your skills/technologies as tags:
    (e.g. Python, React, Docker, AWS, etc.)

12. Work experience (most recent first):
    For each: job title, company, dates, one-sentence description

13. Education:
    Degree, school, year

14. Any certifications?

15. One sentence for your "contact me" section:
    (e.g. "Currently looking for AI engineering roles. Let's talk.")

16. Preferred theme: dark or light?
